Downloading the App on your Android Phone or Tablet

Download the app on your Android phone or tablet.

Either set up a Google account or sign in to your Google account. This is the account you use to access other Google services like Gmail.

Several phones and tablets from different brands are Android devices. These brands include Alcatel, Google, HTC, LG, Moto, Samsung and Sony. Android is the software that these device use to function.

On an Android device, you typically download apps from the Play Store. This is this official, pre-installed program that gives you access to all apps. To know more on how to download an app from the Play Store, see:

Attention: Make sure you are connected to a wireless internet network (Wi-Fi) before you download any apps. Otherwise you will use a lot of data which can result in additional charges from your mobile network service provider.
