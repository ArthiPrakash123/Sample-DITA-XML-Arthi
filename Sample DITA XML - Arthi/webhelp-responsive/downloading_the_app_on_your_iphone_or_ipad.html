Downloading the App on your iPhone or iPad

Download the app on your iPhone or iPad.

You must first either set up an Apple ID account or log in to your Apple ID account – this is the account you use to access all Apple services.

If you are unsure of how to download an app on your iPhone, see: Downloading an app on your iOS device.

If you have an iPhone or iPad, then you typicllly download apps from the App Store. This is an official, pre-installed program on Apple devices that gives you access to all the apps. 

Attention: Make sure you are connected to a wireless internet network (Wi-Fi) before you download any apps. Otherwise you will use a lot of data which can result in additional charges from your mobile network service provider.
  1. Open the App Store in your iPhone or iPad's menu by tapping on the App Store icon.
  2. Search for the app you want to download – either by typing in the search bar at the top, or browsing through the apps on the screen.
  3. Tap on the name of the app
  4. Install the app. Choose one the following:
    • Tap get, if the app is free.
    • Tap on the price if the app is not free. You can then add a payment method through your Apple ID and pay for the app.
    • Click the link provided on the trusted organization’s webpage to download an app. This will take you directly to the App Store.
    Note: If you have a lot of apps on your home screen, you can run out of room for new icons. You must then swipe across the page to see the new icon.
    The app automatically begins to download on your device. Once installed, a shortcut to the app is added to the menu and your home screen.
